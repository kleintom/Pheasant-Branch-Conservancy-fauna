Pheasant Branch Conservancy

Links

  • Friends of Pheasant Branch: Newsletters and Reports including links to:
    • The "Warren report", a 2000 survey of Pheasant Branch Conservancy fauna;
    • Birds of Pheasant Branch Conservancy,
    • Mammals of Pheasant Branch Conservancy, and
    • Butterflies of Pheasant Branch Conservancy, all WDNR publications
  • Survey Results Results of a late 1990's WI DNR insect survey of the remnant prairie on Frederick's Hill, coordinated by Richard Henderson.
  • Digiscoping by Mike McDowell Bird pictures from Pheasant Branch and other locations in the Madison area.
  • Wisconsin Dragonflies and Damselflies "The goal of this survey is to gain more knowledge about the distributions and habitat requirements of our 160+ species of dragonflies and damselflies (Order Odonata)."
  • Wisconsin Butterflies Most complete information on butterflies, but also contains sections on tiger beetles and robber flies.
  • Wisconsin Frog and Toad Survey "The primary purpose of the WFTS is to determine the status, distribution, and long-term population trends of Wisconsin's twelve frog species." Contains links to species accounts and calls.
  • BugGuide.net Pictures and ids submitted by community members. You can visit my bugguide page here - all of my images on bugguide also appear on this page (but not vice versa).
    Click here for an introduction to the bugguide process.
  • PBC Flora A companion website to this one, covering the flora of Pheasant Branch Conservancy.

Note! I'm an amateur, so it's entirely possible that some ids are wrong. Corrections are appreciated (e-mail pbcflora@gmail.com).

I (Tom Klein) am a member of the Friends of Pheasant Branch, but any errors lie solely with me.

Note that for many many insects, identification to species is entirely impossible from these sorts of photos. Thankfully there are also many many for which it is possible.

There are undoubtedly many many more insects at Pheasant Branch than appear on this page - these are just a few I happen to have pictures of.
